Dining on Campus
We’re glad you are here and hope you will enjoy the many different dining options
                           on campus. From Campus Way Cafe, our resident dining facility, to our Dining Pavilion
                           at the University Union, there is something fresh to fit every palate and lifestyle.
                           We have a strong team here at CSU San Marcos and are constantly looking to improve
                           so we can continue to provide you with a world-class dining experience, value, innovation
                           and above all, delicious, healthy food.

Student Meal Plans
We offer per semester Meal Plans that range from eating all meals, everyday on campus
                                 to a small group of pre-paid meals for use when needed. Meal Plans offer pre-paid
                                 meals at a discount, each Meal Plan also includes Dining Dollars.
